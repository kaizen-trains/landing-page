Mission Statement

A mindset towards pushing skillset.

The root Japanese word "Kaizen" means "on-going (or) continuous Improvement." In the context of effective firearm use, this concept drives everything we do. Constantly striving to provide the most relevant and practical information for our student's benefit.

The training we provide pulls from some of the best instructors/competitors in the industry and includes our learnings in the application of their concepts, in an effort to better demonstrate and articulate them to the trainee.

We are constantly looking to simplify the complex and ensure the end-user can confidently apply what they've learned into their personal training.

Whether a competitor, professional, or responsible citizen we believe our approach is universally applicable.
